Our Approach

Thryve acquires profitable businesses using traditional private equity methods, then channels a portion of operating profits into customer loyalty rewards to create alignment between all stakeholders.

Proven Fundamentals

We target established businesses with $8-25M EBITDA, 25%+ margins, and strong unit economics. Every acquisition must stand on its own financial merits before token integration.

Strategic Integration

Blockchain enables a unique loyalty reward model impossible in traditional structures. Business performance directly enhances customer value without the friction of conventional programs.

Network Economics

Each acquisition strengthens the entire portfolio through cross-brand benefits and shared customer base, creating a virtuous cycle where network effects compound to create defensible moats that deepen with every new business.

Why Now

The convergence of institutional blockchain adoption, new regulatory clarity, and existing market inefficiencies creates a unique window for this approach.

Capital Efficiency

Token-based incentives deeply reduce customer acquisition costs while increasing lifetime value, transforming marketing from expense to investment.

Scalable CAC

Cross-brand network effects allow new acquisitions to benefit from existing customer base, dramatically improving acquisition economics over time.

Regulatory Clarity

Recent guidance on utility tokens and rewards programs provides clear framework for baked-in compliance implementation at institutional scale.

Defensible Moat

First-mover advantage in building cross-brand customer loyalty network creates barriers to entry while reducing competitive risk across portfolio.
