Tokenizing Private Equity

Our Approach

Thryve acquires profitable businesses using traditional private equity methods, then converts a portion of profits into token buybacks distributed as customer rewards—creating alignment between all stakeholders.

Proven Fundamentals

We target established businesses with $5-10M EBITDA, 20%+ margins, and strong unit economics. Every acquisition must stand on its own financial merits before token integration.

Strategic Integration

Blockchain enables a unique profit-sharing model impossible in traditional structures. Customer participation reinforces business economics rather than fighting against them.

Network Economics

Each acquisition strengthens the entire portfolio through cross-brand benefits, shared customer base, and compounding network effects that create defensible moats.

Why Now

The convergence of institutional blockchain adoption, regulatory clarity, and market inefficiencies creates a unique window for this approach.

Capital Efficiency

Token-based incentives reduce customer acquisition costs while increasing lifetime value—transforming marketing from expense to investment.

Scalable CAC

Cross-brand network effects allow new acquisitions to benefit from existing customer base, dramatically improving acquisition economics over time.

Regulatory Clarity

Defensible Moat
